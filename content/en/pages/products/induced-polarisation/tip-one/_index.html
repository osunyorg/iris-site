The TIP One is purposely designed for shallow induced polarisation or Resistivity sounding investigations. The TIP One injects a regulated current or regulated voltage into the ground via a simple 12V battery.
This transmitter is generally used together with V-Fullwavers or Elrec Pro receivers.
To simplify operation, the instrument manages the injection current and voltage automatically. When starting, the TIP One automatically operates several safe tests to ensure that the device is not misused.
